NEO Dr. INC. Launches PDO Elastic Thread ‘JAMBER NCL’ to Maximize Volume Enhancement

Medical device company NEO Dr. INC. (CEO Hyeon-ho Kim) announced on the 15th that it has launched the PDO lifting thread ‘JAMBER NCL,’ which features excellent elasticity.

JAMBER is a spring-type ‘Volume-Up PDO Thread’ first introduced by NEO Dr. INC. in 2021. The name “JAMB” refers to a “doorpost” or “vertical pillar.” Compared to existing spring-type PDO threads, the new product offers two key advantages. According to the company, “JAMBER NCL boasts outstanding elasticity, maintaining its original shape even after repeated stretching or significant elongation. Additionally, its increased length allows it to address nasolabial folds, crow’s feet, and jawline contouring more effectively.”

Dr. Young-jin Park, a plastic surgeon who participated in the product’s development, stated, “JAMBER NCL is an step further advanced version of the existing JAMBER series, which provides excellent tissue support. By enhancing its elasticity, this thread delivers lifting and volumizing effect even without barbs (cogs).” He further emphasized, “It is particularly optimized for facial lifting.”

Hyeon-ho Kim, CEO of NEO Dr. INC., explained, “Since developing JAMBER in 2019, our goal has been to create an absorbable suture with elastic lifting capabilities. The launch of JAMBER NCL is the result of that vision, representing an upgraded version of the JAMBER F series.” He added, “Our long-term goal is to develop products that can be applied to all areas of the body.”

Currently, JAMBER NCL is known by various names in domestic and international markets, including JAMBER FST, JAMBER D, and JAMBER LINE. Looking ahead, NEO Dr. INC. plans to expand its product lineup with body-specific JAMBER threads, JAMBER NOSE for nasal bridge contouring, and JAMBER COG, designed specifically for mid-face lifting.
